  PACK PERSONALIZADA
  ------------------

  (veja PACK-PERSONALIZADO.txt)

================================================================================
  INSTALACAO (qualquer pack)
================================================================================

  1) ModVozALN.exe — escolha raiz da pack, cliente system, voice/
  2) Memurai/Redis 127.0.0.1:6379
  3) INICIAR-SERVIDOR-COM-VOZ.bat (Login -> Voice -> GS)
  4) Cliente: L2VoiceInject.exe

  GameServer SEM editar .java:
    -javaagent:libs/l2voice-bridge.jar
